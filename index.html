Our Frequently Asked Questions

  • What digital marketing services does Anasi Marketing offer?
    We offer a full suite of services including social media marketing, search engine marketing (Google Ads), SEO, web design and development, branding and graphic design, video and motion graphics, and printing and brand production for corporate and growth-focused organizations.
  • Do you work with clients outside Kenya?
    Yes. While we are based in Nairobi, Kenya, we regularly collaborate with clients across East Africa, Europe, Asia, and Australia using online meetings, shared workspaces, and clear timelines so distance is never a barrier.
  • How long does it take to design and launch a website?
    Most corporate websites take between 3 and 8 weeks from discovery to launch, depending on the size of the site, content readiness, required integrations, and feedback speed. We share a clear project plan and milestones before we begin.
  • Can you manage both organic and paid social media campaigns?
    Yes. Our social media marketing service includes strategy, content planning, creative design, community management, and paid campaigns across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok, supported by detailed performance reports.
  • What is included in your branding and graphic design services?
    Our branding and design team helps you define or refresh your brand through brand strategy, logo design, visual identity systems, brand guidelines, corporate stationery, and marketing collateral for both digital and print touchpoints.
  • Do you create videos and motion graphics for social media?
    Yes. We produce promotional videos, explainer clips, logo animations, and social media reels that are optimized for platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn to showcase your brand and campaigns.
  • Do you provide ongoing support after a project goes live?
    We offer ongoing retainers for website maintenance, performance optimization, content updates, and continuous digital marketing support so that your brand keeps growing after launch.
  • How can we get a proposal or quotation?
    You can request a custom quote by filling in the contact form, emailing info@anasimarketing.ltd, or calling or WhatsApping us. Share your goals, timelines, and budget range, and we will respond with a tailored proposal.
